About Me

I am an enthusiastic young developer with passion for machine learning and software development.

During my bachelor's in computer technology, I worked at the University of Stavanger on cloud technologies and AI, and wrote my thesis on machine learning in Python for early Alzheimer's detection.

Over four years in public and private roles, I've gained strong software development experience and thrive in collaborative teams, always aiming to deliver real value. At the University of Stavanger, I also taught mathematics and software development, built tools to simplify workflows, and created illustrations to clarify complex concepts.

I enjoy using .NET and React for larger projects, and Python and TypeScript for prototypes, like a music studio app I built with custom libraries for complex file formats and MIDI. I am enthusiastic, and I love sharing ideas with colleagues. I'm passionate about economics, data analysis, statistics, and science, and I'm always eager to grow both professionally and personally.
